Tourist Spots in Asia, Europe and America

Maldives
The Maldives is a tropical paradise that features white sand beaches, crystal-clear water and coral reefs. It is a popular destination for honeymooners and luxury travelers who want to stay in overwater bungalows.

Paris
Paris is the capital of France and one of the most visited cities in the world. It is famous for its iconic landmarks, such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum and the Notre Dame Cathedral. It is also known for its culture, cuisine and fashion.

New York
New York is the largest and most populous city in the United States. It is a global hub for business, entertainment, art and culture. It is home to many famous attractions, such as the Statue of Liberty, the Empire State Building and Times Square.
